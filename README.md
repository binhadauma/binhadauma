### 🧸 Welcome to my page! Who am I ?!  

![til](https://github.com/debfdaumas/debfdaumas/blob/main/honda%20tohru.gif?raw=true)

##### My name is Binha Dauma. I am a neurodivergent, autistic, and queer person. 🙂💗
##### I'm 29 years old, and I'm finishing a degree in Computer Science at [Northern Rio de Janeiro State University](https://uenf.br/portal/) and a degree in Artificial Intelligence at [UniBF University Center](https://www.unibf.com.br/).
##### I am a junior researcher in deep learning and quantum computing, an independent researcher in the ethics and governance of artificial intelligence, as well as a writer and speaker. 🌌🌃
##### I am a science communicator and educator in the fields of astronomy and space exploration. I am also a member of one of the most active astronomy clubs in the world, the [Louis Cruls Astronomical Group](https://www.instagram.com/louiscruls/). 🚀👩‍🚀🔭
##### In addition, I study linguistics, and besides English, which I already speak, I'm learning Mandarin, Korean, Spanish, French, and German.
##### I enjoy listening to pop, epic, classical, and indie music. 🎼 I am passionate about Star Wars, zombies, sci-fi movies, and shoujo anime. 🧟‍♀️🎥
##### I eat a lot! My favorite foods are pizza, French fries, bacon cheeseburgers with cheddar, and Japanese food. I'm also an excellent cook! 🍕🍣🍟🍔
